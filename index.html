Klub Kyak

About us

We are a vibrant, ambitious, and sociable club in the heart of london.

As one of the largest kayak club in the country we offer paddling for begginers all the way to international athletes.

We also offer trips away for a selected amount of club members for a lifetime experience holiday to exotic countries for kayaking with our team.

Let us be your paddling home, join the Kayak Klub Family today!

Casual kyakers

Here in Klub Kyak, the casual kyaters are for people who has no skills above. Here in the casual kyakers we have a very friendly and welcoming community which allows you to enjoy the expereince to its fullest.
